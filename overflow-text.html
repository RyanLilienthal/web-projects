The Collector


We save
Unique among us
Mom saved what "they are wearing"
And everything else
From one decade to the next
What they are wearing filled closets
Then boxes
Then basements

Then mom died
Then a storage unit filled
With images and things of the ages
An archive of existence

Now mom's overflow
Flows into my attic
With my stuff

Stuffing


My mother-in-law hosts Thanksgiving.
Each year she stuffs the turkey
With all sorts of stuff
I help with set up
Which doesn't require stuff

She purges
Her closets
Her boxes
Her basement
Of clutter.
Hence
Unfettered access to tables and chairs
She says she wants to make it easier.
To leave less stuff
When she dies.
