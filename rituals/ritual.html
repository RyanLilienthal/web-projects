Ritual


What are the rituals you engage in to enter/exit the creative space?

A blank canvas feels like the smooth surface of a newly opened jar of peanut butter. Mmmm hmmmm.
That smooth surface doesn't come easily.
For years, I know paint on wooden panels. I love the resistence of the surface.
The ritual starts at Jerry's the candy shop for kids like me.
Scoping the aisles for different sizes and craddle widths.
Then, selecting a gesso.
But, maybe my gesso vats at home will work.
Setting up at table at home, in the basement.
Lather the interior surface of the craddled wooden panel, and the exterior surface.
Let dry.
Sand.
Repeat.
Repeat
Load and level the easel.
Set up the palette, medium, thinner.
Create a tonal underpainting.
Find and lose the subject, again, and again, and again
Layer in hue, tint, and gloss
Destroy, distupt, find, lose, again, and again, and again.
