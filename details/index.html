I start by placing my pen

... and it’s an ordinary pen that I hold between my thumb, index finger, and middle finger.

Placing my pen ... location and tool come together to create. A dot, a line, a shape, a form, an object. A tool of design, make possible ... easier a shift from oral transmission to a place ... placing .. a place of greater solitude. Is it aloneness ... loneliness? Placing pen happens alone .. but not here ... the words are codes, writing in code. Writing inside a code ... about writing in code. Where is my thumb, index finger, and middle finger. My index.com finger ... a code that doesn't need a pen, but that's where the contours of this experience may have begun. As details come together with this coded expression.
